Crypto Tactics for ICOs

Why use crypto tactics for crowdfunding and Indiegogo campaigns?

According to recent statistics, the cumulative amount of money pledged to funding projects on Kickstarter, between July 2012 and August 2018, was around 3.8 billion US dollars. Another 1.5 billion US dollars was raised through Indiegogo.

While, in only the first five months of 2018, ICOs raised $13.7 billion, almost doubling the roughly $7 billion raised in 2017.

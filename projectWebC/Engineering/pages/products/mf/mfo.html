Modular Power Skids
Open frame skids provide the structural base for the electrical system. Power modules including switchgear, UPS, panel boards and transformers are mounted onto skids and placed directly on the server room floor.

Modular Enclosures
A range of modular enclosures are available to protect electrical infrastructure from the external environment. Most enclosure types are suitable for outdoor use, however this depends on the weather rating of the material being used.

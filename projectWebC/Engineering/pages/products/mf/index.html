Modular Fabrication

E+I Engineering’s modular power skid solutions integrate fully assembled switchgear, UPS systems and PDU electrical schemes on a metal skid engineered to the client’s specifications. All equipment is built and tested in our controlled factory environment, giving the customer improved reliability, improved quality and a higher level of vendor accountability. E+I Engineering’s experienced engineering staff work with our customers on every stage of the project from system conception, through detailed design, engineering, system testing and finally installation and commissioning on site. Through our investment in modular power systems, E+I Engineering have reduced deployment speeds by more than 60% compared to a traditional deployment, eliminating the need for costly and timely integration at the construction site.
